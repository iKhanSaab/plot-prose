Webory Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

Webory is a local-first writing application for planning stories and drafting chapters. The app stores your novels, boards, chapters, and theme preference locally on the device where you use it.

What Webory stores

Webory stores writing data and app preferences in local browser or app storage. This codebase does not include user accounts, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or a remote database.

What Webory sends

Webory does not intentionally upload your writing to a remote server. If you use the hosted web version, your hosting provider may still keep standard web-server logs outside the app itself.

Exports and backups

Exported novels and chapters are written directly to files on your device. Those export files are your backup path. If you clear app storage or uninstall the app, locally stored writing may be removed unless you exported a backup first.

Payments

Webory is intended to be sold as a one-time paid app. Purchase processing for app stores is handled by the relevant store platform, not by this web codebase.
