Plot-On Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

Plot-On is a local-first writing application. The web app stores novels, whiteboards, chapters, and settings in your browser's local storage on the device you use.

What the app stores

Plot-On stores your writing data, board data, and theme preference locally in the browser. The app does not include user accounts, analytics, ads, or a remote database in this codebase.

What the app sends

The app does not intentionally transmit your writing data to a remote server. Standard hosting logs from your web host may still exist outside the app itself.

Exports and backups

When you export a novel or chapter, the file is downloaded directly through your browser. You are responsible for storing and backing up exported files.
