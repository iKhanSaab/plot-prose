# Webory Play Store Listing

## App Name

Webory

## Short Description

Local-first story planning and drafting for writers.

## Full Description

Webory helps writers turn scattered ideas into a working story workspace.

Plan scenes on visual boards, draft chapters, group material into folders, and keep your work stored locally on your device. Webory is built for writers who want a focused planning and drafting tool without accounts, subscriptions, or cloud setup.

### What you can do in Webory

- Create multiple novels in one workspace
- Plan scenes and story structure on visual boards
- Draft chapters with multiple draft versions
- Organize boards and chapters into folders
- Search across your writing and planning notes
- Export JSON backups of your work
- Use the app offline after installation

### Local-first by design

Webory stores your work on your device. Your writing is not intentionally uploaded to a remote server by this app.

Important:

- Export backups regularly
- Clearing app storage or uninstalling the app can remove local data
- Webory does not include sync or account recovery in v1
